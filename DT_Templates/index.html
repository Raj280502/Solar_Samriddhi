About Us

At Solar Samriddhi, we believe that every Indian household deserves access to affordable, sustainable energy. We envision a future where harnessing the power of the sun is as simple as flipping a switch.

Our innovative system analyzes your rooftop using satellite imagery, instantly calculating its solar potential. With just a few clicks, discover how many solar panels your roof can accommodate, the electricity they'll produce, and how much you'll save.

We also help you navigate government schemes and incentives, ensuring you maximize your benefits while contributing to a greener planet.
